FAQs

What services do you offer?

+

I specialize in full-stack web development, including frontend design, backend development, database management, and SEO optimization. I create responsive, modern websites that are both visually appealing and functionally robust.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple portfolio site might take 1-2 weeks, while a full e-commerce platform could take 4-6 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide support after launch?

+

Yes! I offer post-launch support including bug fixes, updates, and maintenance. I also provide training on how to manage your website content and can set up ongoing maintenance packages.

Do you create e-commerce websites?

+

Yes, I create fully functional e-commerce websites with features like product catalogs, shopping carts, secure payment processing, inventory management, and order tracking. I can integrate with popular payment gateways and shipping providers.

What technologies do you work with?

+

I work with modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, and various databases. I stay updated with the latest trends to deliver cutting-edge solutions.
